About David

David Botvinnikov is a healthcare executive, entrepreneur, and private equity investor operating at the intersection of strategic vision and operational excellence. Based in Brooklyn, New York, David has spent his career building organizations from the ground up, scaling them with precision, and investing in the next generation of healthcare ventures.

His leadership philosophy is rooted in a simple belief: exceptional organizations are built by exceptional people in exceptional roles. This conviction has shaped every company he has co-founded, every team he has led, and every investment he has made.

Beyond the boardroom, David serves as a Job Placement Advisor with Collective Kindness — because he believes that meaningful work is one of the most powerful forces for human transformation, and that responsibility extends well beyond profit.
